PROLOGUE:

This document will serve as an end to end agreement between the user and the service (Mattcoin). Failure to abide to these terms after account creation could result in account termination and the liquidation of all the assets associated to the mattcoin account in question. Furthermore, we also manifest the right to terminate a mattcoin account if we feel a certain user is abusing our service for any other purpose for that of which it was originally created - a way of payment between individuals in scratch.

Chargebacks I. I.

Mattcoin will serve as a middle-man for payments between individuals. This means we are liable for any fraudulent agreements done through mattcoin and therefore must go through certain steps to protect the assets of a certain individual from fraud.

I. II.

One of the ways we will protect our users is by introducing a system that includes that of a "pending balance". With this all payments received through mattcoin will undergo a 48 hour cooldown before being able to be used again. We have this system in place to give the user enough time to report a fraud before the amount originally sent trickles down to different accounts. This also prevents scammers from having different "Vault Accounts" to avoid the liquidation of their assets for violating the TOS or committing a fraudulent agreement with an individual.

I. III.

The following only applies to written agreements. As stated in (I. II.) we will introduce the system of "Chargebacks" aswell. This is only to be used when fraud has taken place on the scratch.mit.edu platform and is open to interpretation of the public. Failure to provide a scratch link to the original conversation in scratch will result in a chargeback getting denies. This gets at two things: First off any agreements made on an external communication platform other than scratch won't be eligible for a chargeback as it is a direct violation of the scratch community guidelines. Furthermore, we recommend making all agreements on a scratch studio instead of on someone's profile to prevent a scammer from deleting a conversation to avoid being charged back.

Misuse of service II. I.

As stated in the introduction of this agreement, any misuse of this system will result in immediate termination. Here we will mention some of the actions that would result in such however everything is open to interpretation by the service administrators and your actions should not only be limited by what is listed on this document as we manifest the right to terminate your account with no notice for something we feel was abused on our system.

II. II.

Any actions agreed upon the service administrators to be deceptive or abusive will usually result in a strike (usually being the main keyword here since we can still terminate your account for something we consider a mayor offense). Every account starts with a clean record of 0 strikes, however upon reaching 3 strikes your account will be granted with a permanent termination and furthermore your public ip address, which we log for the security of the system, will be banned from the system. These actions include, but are not limited to: fraudulent agreements, hijacking the system for a higher balance, misusing the currency, etc.

II. III.

The value of mattcoin is determined through a miner (see here for more info). Similar to bitcoin, different parties will use a specialized program that will use the computing resources found on your personal pc to solve a bunch of hashing algorithms (mathematical problems). In return the individual will be awarded with the currency, giving the mattcoin value. MTC is not a decentralized currency however, as we feel it is not healthy for the currency to rely on multiple servers that are most likely only going to be running for a couple of hours at a time.

The following includes actions that are restricted with the miner program. One of these includes overloading our API with fraudulent answers for the SHA-256 algorithm (This would be considered a DOS attack). We do not restrict access to alterations of the miner program (we actually encourage it), however we recommend you have knowledge of what you are doing in order to avoid a loss breaking the mining program.

This should be fairly understood by now, but any attempts to take down the system through the API (be dos attacks or anything else) will result in immediate termination of your account and an ip ban.
